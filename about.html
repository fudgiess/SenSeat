The Gulpers’ Restaurant

Located on the Ground Floor of Royale Place Mall along Don Antonio Drive, Commonwealth Avenue in Quezon City, The Gulpers Restaurant is your friendly neighborhood bar and Filipino-focused eatery. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere, it’s the go-to spot for comfort food and relaxed gatherings.

Our Menu & Hospitality

We take pride in serving a variety of classic Filipino dishes and popular appetizers—including fan favorites like chili cheese sticks (“our take on Dynamite” using fresh siling haba and gooey cheese) that are sure to delight. Our cheerful, attentive staff is committed to giving you a hospitable dining experience from start to finish.

Entertainment & Events

What sets us apart is the unique blend of good food and great entertainment—join us for live jamming nights and karaoke sessions, with recurring performances by local bands like S&S Duo and lively Friday-night jams that bring energy and fun to every visit.

Convenience & Service Hours

Whether you’re dropping by for dine-in or need delivery/takeout, The Gulpers’ makes it easy. We’re open every day, generally from 10 AM until 2 AM, and even during the enhanced community quarantine, we continued serving customers via takeout and delivery.
